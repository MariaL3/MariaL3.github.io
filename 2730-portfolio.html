I am super excited for this opporunity and I think it will give me great insight into the federal legal system. I plan to practice child advocacy law in the future. Now a few fun facts about me. I have been coaching gymnastics for the past 3 years and before that I did gymnastics for 10 years. This sport is always somehting I will hold dear to my heart. I love the excitment when a student gets a new skill and how I have been able to watch students grow and succeed for the past few years. I also love the beach! I absolutely love traveling and being able to explore new places.
